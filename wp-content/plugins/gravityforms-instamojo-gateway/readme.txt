### Installation ###

Easy steps to install the plugin:

1. Upload this folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin (WordPress -> Plugins).
3. Now go to the forms page (WordPress -> Forms) and create/edit a form.
4. Add a name, phone, email field each if you don't have them already.
5. Now from under the "Form Settings" drop down menu above the form editor, select "Instamojo" and click on "Add New".
6. On this page you will find all of the configuration options for this payment gateway and enter the account details (See below).

	**API Key**: Enter your API Key. See your merchant account rep for additional information.  
	**Auth Token**: Enter your Auth Token. See your merchant account rep for additional information.

8. Select "Products and Services" as your transaction type and proceed to map all customer related fields with the ones in your form.
9. Click on "Update Settings". That's it!
10. Proceed to test your form by selecting "Sandbox Mode" and entering payment details provided by the payment provider.

That's it! You are ready to accept credit cards with your Instamojo gateway now connected to Gravity Forms.

### Frequently Asked Questions ###

**Is SSL Required to use this plugin?**  
A valid SSL certificate is not required as your customer credit card details are safely entered on pages hosted by Instamojo, however it is recommended to have one. 

**Does the plugin support direct updates from the WP dashboard?**  
Yes. You can navigate to WordPress -> Tools -> Gravity Forms Instamojo License page and activate the license key you received with your order. Once that is done you will be able to directly update the plugin to the latest version from the WordPress dashboard itself.
